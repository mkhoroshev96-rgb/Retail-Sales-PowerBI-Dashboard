# Power BI Dashboards Portfolio

This repository contains a collection of Power BI dashboards built for practical business analytics and data visualization.

The dashboards focus on transforming raw data into clear and useful business insights for decision-making. The main goal is not only visualization, but also analytical interpretation of key metrics, trends, anomalies, and business performance.

The projects include analysis of sales performance, customer behavior, delivery efficiency, regional comparisons, operational metrics, and business KPIs.

## Main areas of analysis

- Sales performance analysis
- Revenue and profitability tracking
- Customer behavior and segmentation
- Delivery time and logistics efficiency
- Country and regional performance comparison
- Product and order structure analysis
- KPI monitoring and dashboard reporting
- Detection of anomalies and business trends

## Tools used

- Power BI
- SQL
- PostgreSQL
- Excel
- Power Query
- DAX (basic level)

## Dashboard Features

- Interactive filters and slicers
- KPI cards and executive summaries
- Time series analysis
- Comparative visualizations
- Geographic analysis
- Trend identification
- Business-focused storytelling

## Purpose

These dashboards were created as part of practical training in Data Analytics and Product Analytics, with a focus on real-world business cases and portfolio development.

The objective is to demonstrate strong analytical thinking, understanding of business metrics, and the ability to present data clearly for business decisions.

More dashboards and case studies will be added continuously.
